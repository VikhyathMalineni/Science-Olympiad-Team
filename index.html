About Our Team

Founded in 2018, the Dougherty Valley High School Science Olympiad team has been pushing the boundaries of scientific exploration and competition for 8 years. Our team consists of dedicated students passionate about various STEM disciplines.

With a structured approach including Varsity and JV teams, we've consistently ranked among the top teams in our region and state for years. With multiple qualifications to the State competition in just a couple of years, our team has been able to quickly prove its competitive excellence.

Team Structure

Varsity Team (Team A)

Our Varsity team consists of 15 experienced members who compete at the highest level in regional and state competitions. These students typically have 2+ years of Science Olympiad experience.

  • Competes in all regional and state events
  • Focuses on advanced scientific concepts
  • Mentors Junior Varsity members
  • Led by team captains and coaches

Junior Varsity (Team B)

Our Junior Varsity team includes 15-20 members who are newer to Science Olympiad but equally passionate about science. This team focuses on building skills and gaining competition experience.

  • Competes in regional competitions and state events
  • Develops foundational scientific knowledge
  • Receives mentoring from Varsity members
  • Prepares for future Varsity positions
