Our Achievements

Celebrating the success and hard work of the Dougherty Valley High School Science Olympiad team.

2025 Season Highlights

Team A

2nd Place at Regionals

Our Varsity team (Team A) secured 2nd place overall at the 2025 Regional Competition, competing against 24 schools from across the region.

12 individual medals earned

This outstanding performance qualified us for the State Finals, where we continued to demonstrate our scientific excellence.

Team B

4th Place at Regionals

Our Junior Varsity team (Team B) achieved an impressive 4th place finish at Regionals, showcasing the depth and strength of our entire program.

8 individual medals earned

This result is particularly impressive as many of our Team B members were first-time competitors.

8th Place at State Finals

Competing against the top 30 schools in the state, our team secured an impressive 8th place finish at the 2025 State Finals.

5 individual medals at the state level

This represents our highest state finish in team history and demonstrates our continued growth and improvement.
