About Our Team

Learn about our history, mission, and what makes the Dougherty Valley High School Science Olympiad team special.

Our History

Founded in 2018, the Dougherty Valley High School Science Olympiad team began with just 12 students and a passionate science teacher who believed in the power of competitive science.

Over the past 7 years, we've grown into a program with over 30 active members across Varsity and Junior Varsity teams, consistently ranking among the top teams in our region.

From our humble beginnings to our current success, we've maintained our commitment to scientific excellence, teamwork, and the joy of discovery.

Our Mission

At Dougherty Valley High School Science Olympiad, we believe that science is not just about facts and formulas, but about curiosity, problem-solving, and collaboration. Our mission is to:

  • Foster a passion for scientific inquiry and discovery
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Build teamwork and communication abilities
  • Create a supportive community of young scientists
  • Prepare students for future success in STEM fields

Through rigorous preparation, friendly competition, and a supportive team environment, we strive to make science exciting and accessible to all students.
